THE EXOSOPTIC

We love Exotic Animals!

Journey with us into the enchanting realm of exotic pets at ExoSopTic, a passion project that began in the heart of Terengganu, Malaysia. What started as a simple love for exotic animals has transformed into the emergence of ExoSopTic as a distinguished name in the Malaysian Exotic Pet trade. Hailing from humble beginnings, our founder, an exotic pet hobbyist, set the stage for ExoSopTic's venture. Today, our operations thrive in the bustling city of Subang Jaya, Selangor, where our showroom proudly showcases a diverse collection of breathtaking exotic pets, redefining the very essence of pet companionship. Explore our website for a comprehensive range of services, from premium exotic pet food to essential equipment, and choose from a delightful array of exotic pets to join your family. Join us at ExoSopTic, where passion meets expertise, and let's embark on this extraordinary journey into the captivating world of exotic pets together.
